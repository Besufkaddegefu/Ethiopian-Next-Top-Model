Our Story

Ethiopian Next Top Model

Founded in 2019, Ethiopian Next Top Model emerged as a visionary platform dedicated to showcasing the extraordinary beauty and talent of Ethiopian women to the world. What began as a dream to create opportunities has blossomed into Africa's most prestigious modeling competition, celebrating diversity, empowering youth, and redefining African beauty standards.

We are more than just a competition – we are a movement. A movement that believes in the power of Ethiopian beauty, the strength of our heritage, and the potential of every young woman who dares to dream. Through rigorous training, international exposure, and professional mentorship, we transform aspiring models into confident, world-class talents ready to conquer global runways.

  • Vision: To position Ethiopia as Africa's leading source of modeling talent
  • Mission: Discover & develop exceptional Ethiopian models globally
  • Values: Excellence, Integrity, Diversity, Empowerment, Cultural Pride

What We Offer

YOUR PATH TO SUCCESS

We provide comprehensive support and opportunities for aspiring Ethiopian models

Runway Excellence & Stage Presence

Develop commanding runway skills that make you stand out on any catwalk. Our expert coaches teach you the nuances of different runway styles—from the powerful walk of haute couture to the commercial appeal of ready-to-wear shows. Learn to project confidence, maintain perfect posture, and master the art of turning and posing that international designers look for.

Photographic Modeling Mastery

Excel in front of the camera with specialized training in editorial, commercial, and high-fashion photography. Learn to convey emotion, tell stories through poses, and work with lighting and angles. Our sessions with renowned photographers help you develop the versatility needed for magazine covers, advertising campaigns, and digital content creation.

Personal Branding & Digital Presence

Build a powerful personal brand that opens doors to international opportunities. We help you identify your unique attributes, develop your signature style, and create a compelling digital presence across social media platforms. Learn how to effectively market yourself to agencies and brands while maintaining authenticity and professionalism.

Industry Readiness & Professionalism

Prepare for the realities of the modeling industry with comprehensive training in professional conduct, contract literacy, financial management, and self-care. We equip you with the business acumen and personal skills needed to build a sustainable career, navigate challenges, and maintain longevity in the competitive world of fashion.
